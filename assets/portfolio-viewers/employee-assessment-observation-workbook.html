Assessment Architecture

  • Competency and activity mapping define what each exercise is intended to surface.
  • Activity sheets pair observable indicators with anchored scoring guidance.
  • Evidence fields capture concrete actions rather than unsupported personality labels.
  • Daily recap sheets consolidate activity evidence before the final score view.

Evidence Boundaries

  • The workbook demonstrates assessment design, observation structure, and scoring logic.
  • Scores reflect the supplied observation and assignment evidence, not independent validation.
  • The public preview withholds participant identity and organization-specific references.
  • The editable workbook is retained for expert review but is not shipped by the public site.

Workflow

1. MapConnect competencies to assessment activities and evidence methods.
2. ObserveRecord activity-specific behavior against anchored indicators.
3. CalibrateReview evidence quality and align scoring across activities.
4. ConsolidateRoll daily evidence into final dimension scores and reporting.
