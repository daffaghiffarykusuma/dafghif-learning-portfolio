Case Flow

The work sequence behind the grouped artifacts.

  1. Explore ideas

    Guide mentees from individual SDG interests into shared problem statements and early project directions.

  2. Develop the concept

    Use voting, feasibility checks, and project canvas prompts to converge on one workable concept.

  3. Prepare the presentation

    Track evidence, ownership, storytelling, and slide readiness before the final pitch.

  4. Package the pitch

    Use a structured deck template to present problem, evidence, solution, impact, implementation, and sustainability.
