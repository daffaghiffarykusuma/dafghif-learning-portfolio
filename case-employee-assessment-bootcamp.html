Case Flow

The work sequence behind the grouped artifacts.

  1. Map assessment evidence

    Connect behavior dimensions to activities, methods, indicators, and anchored rubrics.

  2. Capture observable behavior

    Record concrete actions and statements across discussions, assignments, coaching, simulations, and peer review.

  3. Calibrate and consolidate

    Review evidence quality, align scoring, and combine activity results into dimension-level findings.

  4. Translate results

    Convert the consolidated evidence into a concise individual report with scores, strengths, and development priorities.
